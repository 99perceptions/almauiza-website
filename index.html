About Us

Al Mauiza Foundation is a registered non-profit organization under Section 42 of the Companies Act (2017), dedicated to reviving the real values of Islam in light of the Holy Qur'an.

We believe that the Qur'an was revealed for reflection, not mere recitation. Yet, for many Muslims around the world, the message remains distant — veiled by language barriers and lack of access to structured learning.

Through education, research, and community empowerment, we strive to create a world where the Qur'an once again becomes the center of life, guidance, and unity for the Ummah.

A Movement of Comprehension

At Al Mauiza Foundation, we are building a community where every Muslim can connect directly with the words of Allah, understand their meaning, and apply them in daily life.

The Qur'an is the most read and memorized book in human history — yet, paradoxically, it remains one of the least understood by its own followers.

This lack of comprehension is not a matter of ignorance — it is a matter of access. Al Mauiza Foundation exists to change that reality.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of authentic knowledge and constructive dialogue, inspiring individuals and communities to live by the timeless wisdom and ethical principles of the Qur'an.

Our Mission

We promote universal values of compassion, integrity, and wisdom through education, research, and community engagement. We aim to nurture an enlightened and harmonious society guided by the shared values of faith and reason.

The Problem We Are Solving

Across the globe, millions recite the Qur'an beautifully but struggle to comprehend its meaning. This disconnect has silently shaped a generation that reads divine revelation without realizing its depth, guidance, and transformative power.

Understand Qur'an Global (UQG)

Our flagship initiative — a global movement that teaches the Qur'an in an easy, structured, and interactive way.

🎓 Modern Methodology

Through modern pedagogical methods, including the TPI (Total Physical Interaction) teaching approach, multimedia tools, and trained instructors, UQG enables students to understand the Qur'an and Salah word-for-word.

🌍 Global Reach

UQG's programs are currently empowering learners in over 40 countries, offering both online and on-ground learning opportunities through Centers of Excellence and digital platforms.

Key Programs

✓ Understand Qur'an & Salah Courses (Levels 1–5)
✓ Teacher Training & Certification Programs
✓ Children's Qur'an Education Initiative
✓ Centers of Excellence (CoE) across Pakistan
✓ Online and Global Classes via LMS & Mobile App

Research & Dialogue

Beyond teaching, the Foundation promotes intellectual dialogue and collaborative research on contemporary Islamic thought.

We host seminars, conferences, and think-tank discussions that bridge classical knowledge with modern realities — encouraging scholars, academics, and professionals to explore how Qur'anic values can inform ethical governance, social harmony, and responsible leadership in today's world.

Our goal is to make the Qur'an not only recited in homes but reflected in decisions, institutions, and societies.
